What is Aikido?

Aikido is a Japanese martial art that teaches self-defense without the need to harm your opponent. In our school, we emphasize:

  • Effective self-defense - techniques based on biomechanics, not physical strength
  • Physical development - improved fitness, flexibility, and coordination
  • Mindfulness - stress reduction, concentration, body awareness
  • Etiquette and culture - traditional Japanese approach to martial arts

How to Start Aikido Training in Gdynia?

1

Come to Your First Class

Arrive 15 minutes early in comfortable sportswear (sweatpants, leggings, t-shirt). Keikogi (Aikido uniform) can be purchased later.

2

Learn the Basics

You'll learn etiquette, warm-up exercises (Aiki Taiso), and first techniques. You'll practice with an experienced aikidoka as your partner.

3

Continue Regularly

Minimum 2 times per week is a good start. Consistency is more important than intensity.

Is Aikido effective for self-defense?

Yes! Aikido teaches effective defensive techniques. In Japan, Aikido is officially taught to elite police units. Learn more about benefits here.

Can I train at X age / with a health condition?

Aikido is accessible to people from ages 12-13 with no upper age limit. We adapt techniques to individual capabilities. Consult your doctor if you have health concerns.
