## Photo Guidelines

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format**: Square (1:1) or Portrait (4:5) preferred
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1080x1080px
- **Lighting**: Natural light preferred, avoid harsh shadows
- **Focus**: Sharp, clear faces and movements

### Content Guidelines:
- Always get permission before posting photos with identifiable people
- Avoid posting children's faces (privacy)
- Show technique clearly - good angles
- Capture energy and movement
- Include both action and calm moments
- Show diversity of students (all ages, genders if applicable)

### What to Avoid:
- Blurry or poorly lit photos
- Messy dojo backgrounds
- Photos during water breaks (unprofessional)
- Injuries or accidents
- People looking tired or unflattering angles
